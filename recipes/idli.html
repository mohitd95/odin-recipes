Idli

Description

Idli is a soft, fluffy steamed rice cake made from fermented rice and urad dal batter.

It is a staple South Indian breakfast item served with chutney and sambar.

Ingredients

  • Rice – 1 cup
  • Urad dal – 1/4 cup
  • Salt – to taste
  • Water – as needed

Steps

  1. Soak rice and urad dal separately for 4-6 hours, then grind to a smooth, fluffy batter.
  2. Combine both, adjust water, and ferment overnight.
  3. Add salt and gently mix the batter.
  4. Pour into greased idli molds and steam for 10-15 minutes until soft and fluffy.
