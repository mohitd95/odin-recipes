Uttapam

Description

Uttapam is a thick, soft pancake made from the same fermented batter as dosa.

It is topped with chopped vegetables like onions, tomatoes, and green chilies.

Ingredients

  • Dosa batter – 1 cup
  • Onion (chopped) – 1/4 cup
  • Tomato (chopped) – 1/4 cup
  • Green chili (chopped) – 1 tbsp

Steps

  1. Heat a pan and pour a ladleful of dosa batter to form a thick pancake.
  2. Sprinkle chopped onions, tomatoes, and green chilies over the top.
  3. Drizzle oil around the edges and cook on medium heat.
  4. Flip if desired and cook the other side lightly, then serve hot.
