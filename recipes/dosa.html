Dosa

Description

Dosa is a thin, crispy South Indian crepe made from fermented rice and urad dal batter.

It is typically served hot with coconut chutney and tangy sambar.

Ingredients

  • Rice – 1 cup
  • Urad dal – 1/4 cup
  • Salt – to taste
  • Water – as needed

Steps

  1. Soak rice and urad dal for 4-6 hours, then grind into a smooth batter with water.
  2. Ferment the batter overnight or for at least 8 hours.
  3. Add salt and mix well before using.
  4. Pour a thin layer of batter on a hot tawa and cook until golden and crisp.
